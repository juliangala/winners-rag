# Universidad CEU San Pablo

## Overview

Private university located in Madrid, Spain, offering one of the most competitive Medicine programs among private universities in the country.

It combines a structured admission process with multiple exam sittings, strong demand, and a well-established academic environment, making it a key strategic option in most application portfolios.

### Strategic insight (EXAM STRUCTURE)

👉 Exam only includes:

- Biology (1º Bachillerato)  
- Chemistry (1º Bachillerato)  

Advantages:

- Lower syllabus vs competitors  
- No Physics or Maths  
- Faster preparation  
- Ideal for retake strategy  

---

### Additional scoring (CEU internal advantage)

Candidates can obtain up to **+2 additional points** on top of their admission score.

Breakdown:

- Up to 1.5 points for studying in CEU institutions:
  - 0.25 per year of Bachillerato (max 0.5)  
  - 0.1 per year ESO (max 0.4)  
  - 0.1 per year Primary (max 0.6)  

- CFGS in CEU: +0.45 points  

- Health sciences degree (CEU): +1 point  

- Family link with CEU:
  - 0.1 per relative (max 0.5)  

- Child of CEU staff / affiliated hospitals: +0.5  

- Disability: up to +0.5  

👉 Max total: **+2 points**
