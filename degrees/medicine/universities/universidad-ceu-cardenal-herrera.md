# Universidad CEU Cardenal Herrera

## Overview

Private university located in the Valencian Community (Spain), with Medicine programs mainly based in Castellón and Valencia.

It offers a structured admission process, bilingual academic options, and strong international pathways, making it a solid option within a multi-university Medicine application strategy.

---

## Quick evaluation

- Competitiveness level: Medium – High  
- University type: Established private university  
- Strategic role: Core / Safety option  

---

## Key facts

- Type: Private university  
- Location: Valencian Community  
- Main campuses: Castellón, Valencia (Alfara del Patriarca)  
- Language: Spanish / Bilingual  
- Medicine program start: September  
- Number of seats: ~120 total  
  - Castellón: 70 (Spanish)  
  - Valencia: 50 (Bilingual)  
- International exposure: High  
- Admission start: November (approx.)

## Program characteristics

### Curriculum structure

- Duration: 6 years (360 ECTS)  

- Years 1–2: Theoretical foundation  
- Years 3–6: Clinical training  

---

### Teaching methodology

- Combination of theoretical and practical learning  
- Small group teaching  
- Personalized tutoring from year 1  
- Strong focus on mentoring  

---

### Clinical training

- Simulation-based training before hospital exposure  
- Clinical rotations start in Year 4  
- Final year fully practical  

Hospital network includes:

- La Fe  
- Hospital General de Valencia  
- Peset  
- Arnau de Vilanova  
- Manises  
- Private hospitals (Quirón, IMED)  

---

### International recognition

- WFME accreditation (since 2023)  

---

### International opportunities

- Clinical rotations abroad (Germany, Switzerland, USA)  

**EPOCA Program:**

- Rotations in New York  
- Preparation for USMLE  
- Scholarships available  

---

### MIR preparation

- Integrated into the degree  
- Starts progressively from Year 3  
- Intensive preparation in final year  

---

### Additional programs

- Optional Diploma in Clinical and Diagnostic Medicine

## Admission model

### Selection filter

- Minimum requirement: >7/10 in Year 1 Bachillerato  
- Must be taking at least 2 of:
  - Biology  
  - Chemistry  
  - Physics  
  - Mathematics II  
- Internal admission exam required  

---

### Admission test

- **EPAM** → national students  
- **EPOAM** → international students (online)  

---

### Exam structure

- Duration: 120 minutes  
- 75 multiple-choice questions  

Distribution:

- Biology: 35  
- Chemistry: 15  
- Physics/Mathematics: 15  
- Scientific reading comprehension: 10  

---

### Exam characteristics

- No calculator allowed  
- No negative marking  
- Can be taken in English (international candidates)  

---

### Evaluation model

#### Castellón

- Academic record: 60%  
- Exam: 40%  

---

#### Valencia (Bilingual)

- Academic record: 60%  
- Exam: 35%  
- English level: up to +5%  

---

### English scoring bonus (Valencia)

- B1 → 0  
- B2 → +0.2  
- C1 → +0.4  
- C2 → +0.5  

---

### Complementary points (MAX +2)

- CEU school education → up to +1.5  
- CEU vocational training → +0.45  
- CEU university degree → up to +1  
- Family connection → up to +0.5  
- Child of CEU staff → +1  
- Local residency → +0.5  

---

### Admission flexibility

- Two official exam sittings  
- Best score considered  
- Retake allowed
