# Universidad Europea de Madrid

## Overview

Private university located in Madrid, Spain, offering one of the most established private Medicine programs in the country.

It is part of the Universidad Europea group and is known for its strong clinical network and high demand compared to other private universities.

## Admission model

### Selection filter

- Strict requirement: Health Sciences academic background  
- Applications from other academic tracks are not considered  
- Minimum Spanish level: B2 (for non-native speakers)
- More selective than other Universidad Europea campuses

---

### Evaluation model

**Weighted components:**

- Year 12 (1º Bachillerato) academic average: 90%  
- English language test (if non-native): 10%  

**Required (non-weighted):**

- Talent UE test (competency-based)  
- Spanish language test (if non-native)

## Admission process

1. Application submission  
2. Document upload  
   - Spanish students: DNI + Year 12 grades  
   - International students: passport + grade equivalency report  
3. Payment of exam fee (€150)  
4. Online admission tests  
5. Admission decision:
   - Accepted  
   - Waiting list  
   - Rejected  
6. If accepted:
   - Payment of seat deposit + administrative fee within 5 working days  
   - Deposit is non-refundable unless study visa is denied (non-Spanish nationals) 

---

## Admission timeline

Multiple admission batches between March and July.

- Final admission round takes place in July  
- Limited seats → early application strongly recommended
