# Universidad CEU Abat Oliba

## Overview

Private university located in Barcelona (Spain), part of the CEU educational group.

It offers a newly launched Medicine program (starting 2026–2027), with a strong focus on small-group teaching, humanistic values, and early integration into the healthcare system.

The program is built around a new health sciences campus in Esplugues de Llobregat, positioned within a growing biomedical hub.
