# Universidad Europea de Canarias

## Overview

Private university located in La Orotava, Tenerife (Canary Islands, Spain), offering the Medicine degree.

It is a recent option within the Spanish university system, with lower competition compared to more established private universities.

## Admission process

1. Application submission  
2. Document upload  
   - Spanish students: DNI + Year 12 grades  
   - International students: passport + grade equivalency report  
3. Payment of admission fee (€65)  
4. Online admission tests  
5. Admission decision:
   - Accepted  
   - Waiting list  
   - Rejected  
6. If accepted:
   - Payment of seat deposit + administrative fee within 5 working days  
   - Deposit is non-refundable unless study visa is denied (non-Spanish nationals) 

---

## Admission timeline

Multiple admission batches between March and July.

- Final admission round takes place in July  
- Limited seats → early application strongly recommended
