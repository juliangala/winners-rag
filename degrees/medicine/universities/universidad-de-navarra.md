# Universidad de Navarra

## Overview

Private university located in Pamplona (Spain), widely recognized as one of the most prestigious and selective institutions in Spain for Medicine.

It offers a highly rigorous academic program with strong clinical training, international recognition, and one of the most demanding admission processes among private universities.

---

## Quick evaluation

- Competitiveness level: Very High  
- University type: Top-tier private university  
- Strategic role: Ambitious option (A)  

---

## Key facts

- Type: Private university  
- Location: Pamplona, Spain  
- Language: Spanish  
- Additional track: International Program in Medicine  
- Medicine program start: September  
- Number of seats: ~200  
- International exposure: high  

---

## Program characteristics

### Curriculum structure (Integrated Curriculum)

The Medicine program follows an **integrated curriculum model** where subjects are not taught in isolation but interconnected throughout the degree.

Key characteristics:

- Integration of basic sciences and clinical subjects from the beginning  
- Learning in clinical context rather than separated theoretical blocks  
- Reduction of redundancy across subjects  
- Progressive and structured knowledge development  

---

### Teaching methodology

- Active and interactive methodologies (case-based learning, problem-based learning)  
- Continuous assessment system  
- Strong emphasis on clinical reasoning  
- Student-centered learning approach  

---

### Clinical training

- Clinical exposure from **year 1**  
- Use of **simulation centers** for early practical training  
- Progressive responsibility in clinical environments  
- Strong integration with hospital system  

---

### Clinical network

- Main hospital: Clínica Universidad de Navarra  
- Integrated biomedical campus (with CIMA research center)  
- High-quality and structured clinical exposure  

---

### International recognition

- WFME accredited program  
- Enables access to medical residency in:
  - USA  
  - Canada  
  - Australia  

---

### Academic outcomes

- ~43% of students rank within top 2000 in MIR exam  
- Strong historical performance in residency placement  

---

### Program structure highlights

- Total credits: 360 ECTS  
- Duration: 6 years  

#### Key milestone

  - Specialties include:
    - Primary care  
    - Surgery  
    - Pediatrics  
    - Gynecology  
    - Psychiatry  
    - Emergency medicine

- Option to complete rotations in:

## Admission model

### Selection filter

- Very strong academic profile required  
- Minimum academic requirement:
  - **≥ 7/10 in Bachillerato (or equivalent)**  
- Highly selective process  
- Limited number of seats  

---

### Evaluation model

#### Standard weighting:

- Admission exam: 70%  
- Academic record (Year 1 Bachillerato): 30%  

---

## Admission pathways

### 1. Standard exam (Spain-based students)

- Applies to:
  - Students studying in Spain  
- Single pathway:
  - **March exam (mandatory)**  

---

### 2. International students (studying outside Spain)

Students must choose **one pathway only**:

#### Option A: December exam (online)

- Requires ISAT completed beforehand  
- Faster decision timeline  
- Higher competition  

#### Option B: March pathway

Two alternatives:

- Online (with ISAT)  
- In-person exam in Spain (**no ISAT required**)  

---

### Strategic insight (ONLY international students)

- High ISAT → ISAT pathway can be advantageous  
- Lower ISAT → better to take in-person exam (March)  
- December = earlier result but less preparation time  
- March = more preparation time (especially relevant for some international calendars)  

---

### 3. International Baccalaureate (IB pathway)

Students following IB do **not take the admission exam**.

Requirements:

- Predicted score: **≥ 37 points**  
- Minimum:
  - Biology HL ≥ 6  
  - Chemistry HL ≥ 6  

Additional requirement:

- Personal interview  

---

## Admission process

1. Registration in miUNAV portal  
2. Application form completion  
3. Document upload:
   - ID / passport  
   - Academic transcripts  
   - Photo  
   - Recommendation letter (international students)  
4. Payment of admission fee (~€100)  
5. Admission evaluation:
   - Exam OR IB pathway  
6. Admission decision:
   - Admitted  
   - Waiting list  
   - Rejected  
7. If admitted:
   - Enrollment within deadline
