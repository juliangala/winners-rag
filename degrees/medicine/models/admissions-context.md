# Admissions context for Medicine in Spain

## Objective

This document defines the different access pathways to Medicine degrees in Spain depending on the student's educational background.

It allows the system to adapt recommendations based on:

- Country of origin
- Education system
- Access route to Spanish universities

---

## Core principle

The same academic level does not translate equally across different education systems.

Admission strategy must consider:

- Recognition of qualifications
- Required exams (if any)
- Weight of academic record

---

## Main student contexts

### 1. Spanish system (EvAU)

Students who completed Bachillerato in Spain.

#### Characteristics

- Access through EvAU
- Final grade based on:
  - Bachillerato
  - Selectividad exams
- Direct competition based on cut-off grades

#### Implications

- Full access to public universities
- Highly competitive
- Strong dependency on final exam performance

---

### 2. EU / equivalent systems

Students from EU countries or equivalent agreements.

#### Characteristics

- Grade conversion to Spanish scale
- May require specific exams depending on pathway

#### Implications

- Access to public universities possible
- Complexity in grade conversion
- Strategy depends on equivalence rules

---

### 3. International students (non-EU)

Students from non-EU systems.

#### Subtypes

##### a. International system aligned with EU (e.g. French system abroad)

- May have partial recognition
- Sometimes easier integration

---

##### b. Non-equivalent systems

- Require:
  - Credential recognition (homologación)
  - PCE exams (UNED)

---

#### Implications

- More complex process
- Longer timelines
- Public university access more difficult
- Private universities more accessible

---

## Strategic implications

### Public universities

- Favor students with:
  - Direct access (EvAU or equivalent)
  - Strong converted grades

---

### Private universities

- More flexible across all systems
- Often do not depend on official equivalence
- Strong alternative for international students

---

## Key variables to consider

- Country of origin
- Education system (Spanish, French, Moroccan, IB, etc.)
- Grade conversion
- Need for additional exams (PCE)
- Timeline for validation

---

## Usage in recommendation logic

This document allows the system to:

- Identify the correct admission pathway
- Adjust expectations based on the system
- Recommend realistic strategies based on origin

The system must always consider:

> Access pathway before competitiveness
