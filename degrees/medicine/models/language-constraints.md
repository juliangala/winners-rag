# Language requirements for Medicine admission

## Objective

Define how language proficiency affects eligibility and competitiveness for Medicine degrees in Spain.

---

## Core principle

Language is a constraint, not a performance indicator.

It determines:

- Which universities the student can apply to
- Which programs are accessible
- The student’s ability to succeed in the program

The system must evaluate language before making recommendations.

---

## Spanish language

### Importance

Spanish is the primary language of instruction in:

- Public universities
- Most private universities

---

### Required level

- Typically B2–C1 minimum
- Higher levels improve academic performance and adaptation

---

### Implications

- Students without sufficient Spanish level:
  - Cannot realistically access most public universities
  - Must prioritize:
    - improving Spanish
    - private universities with support or flexibility

---

## English language

### Importance

English is relevant in:

- Private universities
- Bilingual or English-track programs

---

### Advantages

- Access to:
  - bilingual programs
  - international tracks
- Competitive advantage in some universities

---

### Implications

- Strong English level expands available options
- May compensate for other limitations in some cases

---

## Language profiles

### 1. Native or fluent Spanish

- Full access to:
  - public universities
  - private universities

---

### 2. Intermediate Spanish (B1–B2)

- Limited access to public universities
- Must evaluate readiness carefully
- Private universities more viable

---

### 3. Low Spanish level (<B1)

- Public universities generally not viable
- Must prioritize:
  - private universities
  - English or bilingual programs
  - language improvement pathway

---

### 4. Strong English profile

- Access to:
  - bilingual programs
  - international tracks
- Advantage in private universities

---

## Strategic implications

- Language can override academic potential
- Strong academic profile is not sufficient without language compatibility
- Language must be evaluated before competitiveness

---

## Usage in recommendation logic

The system must:

- Filter universities based on language compatibility
- Avoid recommending inaccessible programs
- Highlight when language is a limiting factor

The system must always consider:

> Eligibility before competitiveness
